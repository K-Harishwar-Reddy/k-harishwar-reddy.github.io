About Me

Researcher in AI, digital pathology, and biomedical foundation models.

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Florida. My research focuses on developing and evaluating machine learning models for biomedical imaging, especially kidney digital pathology.

My current interests include self-supervised learning, histopathology foundation models, multi-modal biomedical AI, spatial transcriptomics, segmentation, and clinical prediction from whole slide images.
